# Freemium Rules

## Free Tier

Free users receive the real Morning Fax experience, limited by depth rather than by dignity.

Free includes:

- Account required.
- One morning fact per day.
- Optional one evening fact per day.
- Last 7 days of archive access.
- Share card image.
- Save cards.
- Category selection: Everything or selected categories.

Free excludes:

- More than one fact per delivery.
- Longer premium card body.
- Visible source attribution.
- Full archive.
- Expanded category palette if reserved for premium.
- Category blacklist.

## Premium Tier

Premium adds depth and control.

Premium includes:

- Up to three facts per delivery.
- Longer form card content.
- Visible source attribution.
- Full archive.
- Expanded category palette.
- Category blacklist.

## Upgrade Moments

The premium offer appears during onboarding and remains available through natural upgrade moments:

- Tapping source attribution on a free card.
- Reaching beyond the 7-day archive.
- Asking for more facts today.
- Opening expanded categories.
- Opening category blacklist controls.
- Settings upgrade entry point.

Upgrade prompts should be quiet and editorial. They should not use urgency, streak pressure, countdowns, or shame.

## Fallback Behavior

If a trial ends without conversion, the user falls back to free. Saved data remains attached to the account, but premium-only access disappears until the user subscribes again.
